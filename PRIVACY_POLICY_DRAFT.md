# Home Floorplan Privacy Policy

Effective date: [DATE]

Home Floorplan helps you view, organize, and control compatible Apple Home/HomeKit accessories using a visual floorplan, automation tools, environmental context, and optional AI-assisted features.

This policy explains what data the app can access, what stays on your device, and what may be sent to external providers only when you choose to enable those features.

## Summary

- Home Floorplan requires Apple Home/HomeKit permission for core smart home functionality.
- Floorplans, markers, preferences, local history, insights, and app settings are stored locally on your device.
- AI features are optional. If enabled, selected home context and prompts may be sent to the AI provider you configure.
- Home Floorplan does not sell your data.
- Home Floorplan does not use advertising tracking.
- Home Floorplan does not bundle your personal AI API keys.

## Data The App May Access

Depending on the permissions you grant and the features you use, Home Floorplan may access the following data.

### Apple Home And HomeKit Data

After you grant Apple Home permission, the app may read and use:

- Home names and selected home information.
- Room names and room assignments.
- Accessory names, categories, services, and capabilities.
- Accessory states, such as on/off state, brightness, lock state, climate mode, cover position, battery state, and reachability.
- HomeKit scenes, automations, triggers, conditions, and actions.
- HomeKit sensor values, including compatible temperature, humidity, air quality, occupancy, light, security, contact, and environmental readings.

This data is used to display your home, control accessories, create or edit scenes and automations, build dashboards, and generate local context.

### Floorplan And User-Created Content

The app may store:

- Floorplan images or scans you import or create.
- Marker positions and accessory placements.
- Room mapping information.
- Automation drafts, scene drafts, rule drafts, notes, settings, and preferences.
- Chat or assistant prompts you enter when using AI features.

### Environmental And Activity Data

The app may store local summaries or logs such as:

- Sensor reading history.
- Accessory activity events.
- Automation or action effectiveness events.
- Environmental insights.
- Habit or routine patterns inferred from local HomeKit context.

These records are used to power dashboards, suggestions, and local app behavior.

### Location Data

If you grant location permission, Home Floorplan may use your location for:

- Home location context.
- Weather and environmental context.
- Map-based home/location setup.
- Geofence setup where available.

The first release is designed to use When In Use location permission and does not require Always location permission.

### Photos, Camera, Microphone, And Speech

Home Floorplan may request access to:

- Photos or files when you choose to import a floorplan image.
- Camera or room scanning features when you choose to create a floorplan from a scan.
- Microphone or speech recognition when you choose to use voice input features.

These permissions are requested only for user-triggered features.

### Notifications

If you enable notifications, the app may store notification preferences and use local context to send relevant alerts or reminders.

## Local Storage

Home Floorplan is local-first. App data such as floorplans, markers, preferences, local history, generated insights, and settings is stored on your device unless a feature explicitly sends selected context to an external service.

Deleting the app from your device may delete locally stored Home Floorplan data. Installing an update over an existing installation normally keeps local app data, subject to iOS behavior.

HomeKit homes, accessories, scenes, and automations are managed by Apple Home/HomeKit. Removing local Home Floorplan data does not necessarily remove HomeKit data from Apple Home.

## Optional AI Features

AI features are optional and require user configuration.

If you enable or configure an AI provider, Home Floorplan may send selected information to that provider to generate responses, explanations, summaries, or action suggestions. This may include:

- Your prompt or voice-transcribed request.
- Relevant room names.
- Relevant accessory names, types, states, and capabilities.
- Relevant HomeKit scene or automation context.
- Relevant sensor summaries or environmental readings.
- Relevant local insight, habit, or action-effectiveness context.

AI providers may process this information according to their own terms and privacy policies. Do not enable AI features if you do not want selected home context or prompts processed by the configured provider.

Home Floorplan does not bundle your personal AI API key in the app. If the app supports user-provided API keys, they are intended to be entered by you and stored securely on the device, such as in Keychain.

## Data Sharing

Home Floorplan does not sell your data.

Data may be processed by:

- Apple services required for HomeKit, platform functionality, TestFlight, App Store distribution, iOS diagnostics, or crash reporting.
- AI providers you choose to configure and use.
- Other service providers only if added in a future version and disclosed before release.

Home Floorplan does not use advertising SDKs or advertising tracking in the current release.

## Tracking

Home Floorplan does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising or data broker purposes.

If tracking, advertising SDKs, or third-party analytics are added in the future, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

## Data Retention And Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until:

- You delete it using app features where available.
- The app prunes older records where retention behavior is implemented.
- You delete the app from your device.

To remove Home Floorplan local data, delete data from the app where available or delete the app from your device. HomeKit data managed by Apple Home may need to be removed separately in Apple Home.

## Children

Home Floorplan is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated as Home Floorplan changes. The effective date will be updated when material changes are made.
